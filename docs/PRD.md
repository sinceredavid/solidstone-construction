# SolidStone Construction Landing Page PRD (1-Page)

## Purpose
Create a clear, trustworthy landing page that explains who SolidStone Construction is, what services are offered, and how prospects can request a quote.

## Goals
- Increase quote requests from homeowners and small commercial clients.
- Build trust through proof (licenses, insurance, past work).
- Explain services at a glance and reduce friction to contact.

## Non-Goals
- No client portal or scheduling system.
- No blog or SEO content hub.
- No user accounts or complex forms.

## Target Audience
- Homeowners planning renovations or repairs.
- Small business owners needing general contracting.
- Property managers seeking reliable contractors.

## Key Messages
- Reliable, licensed, and insured general contractor.
- On-time, on-budget delivery with clear communication.
- Free estimates and fast response.

## Pages and Content
### Home
- Hero: headline, value prop, primary CTA (Get a Free Quote) + phone.
- Service highlights: 4-6 cards.
- Process: 3 steps (Consult -> Estimate -> Build).
- Proof: testimonials or project stats.
- CTA band: repeat CTA.
- Footer: contact, service area, hours.

### Services
- Short intro paragraph.
- Service list with 1-2 sentence blurbs (remodeling, roofing, concrete, framing, finishing, repairs).
- Service area note.
- CTA.

### Projects
- 6-9 project cards (image, type, location).
- Optional before/after or quick stats.
- CTA.

### About
- Company story (short).
- Values: safety, quality, communication.
- Licenses/insurance badges.
- CTA.
